# Moonso Link 🌾

**Your Digital Farmer Assistant & Marketplace for Cameroon**

Moonso Link connects farmers, buyers, and agricultural value chain actors across Cameroon — all through WhatsApp and Telegram. List products, search for what you need, check market prices, and get farming advice, all from your phone.

> Created by **CODE::DEV**

---

## The Problem We Solve

Cameroonian farmers struggle to find buyers for their produce, while buyers struggle to find quality products at fair prices. Information is scattered, middlemen take large cuts, and there's no central platform for agricultural trade.

**Moonso Link fixes this.** A free, AI-powered marketplace that works on the messaging apps Cameroonians already use.

---

## Key Features

### 🌾 Marketplace
- **Farmers** list products with quantity, price, location, and photos
- **Buyers** search by product, town, region, or price
- **Market prices** shown alongside listings for fair trading
- **Interested buyers** can express interest — the farmer gets notified instantly

### 🔍 Smart Search
- Natural language search: *"Find tomatoes in Douala"*
- Browse by price: *"Maize under 200 XAF"*
- See where products are sold: *"Where is corn being sold?"*
- View full listing details with farmer name, phone, and description

### ✅ Farmer Verification
- Farmers submit a selfie + ID card photo
- Admin reviews and verifies the account
- Verified farmers' listings are visible to all buyers
- Builds trust in the marketplace

### 📋 Account Management
- View your profile, role, and verification status
- Upgrade from buyer to farmer
- Update your name or region
- See and manage your own listings

### 🏥 Community Features
- **Report issues** — disease outbreaks, product shortages
- **Post farming problems** — pests, soil issues, weather difficulties
- **Give and receive advice** — solutions from the community
- **View active alerts** — disease warnings, shortage notices in your area

### 🎙️ Voice Support
- Send voice messages instead of typing
- Automatically transcribed and processed

### 🔗 Cross-Platform
- Link your WhatsApp and Telegram accounts
- Use Moonso Link seamlessly on both platforms

## How It Works

### For Buyers
1. **Send a message** to Moonso Link on WhatsApp or Telegram
2. **Search for products** naturally — *"Find corn in Yaounde"*
3. **Browse results**, use next/previous to navigate pages
4. **View details** — *"Show details of listing #3"* to see full info
5. **Express interest** — *"Interested in listing #2"* — the farmer gets your contact

### For Farmers
1. **Start selling** — *"I want to sell 50kg of corn at 200 XAF"*
2. **Add optional details** — location, photos, description
3. **Verify your account** — send *"verify my account"* and upload selfie + ID
4. **Get notified** when buyers express interest in your listings
5. **Manage listings** — update price, edit info, delete
